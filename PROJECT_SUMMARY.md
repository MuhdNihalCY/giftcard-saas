# Gift Card SaaS Platform - Project Summary

**Status:** ✅ Production Ready  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Date:** December 2024

---

## Quick Overview

**What:** A comprehensive multi-tenant SaaS platform for digital gift card management  
**Who:** Merchants (businesses), Customers (end users), Administrators (platform managers)  
**Status:** Complete, cleaned, optimized, and ready for production

---

## What This Platform Does

Enables businesses to:
1. **Create** digital gift cards with custom branding
2. **Sell** gift cards through multiple payment gateways
3. **Deliver** gift cards via email, SMS, or PDF
4. **Track** sales, redemptions, and analytics
5. **Manage** payouts and commissions

Enables customers to:
1. **Browse** and purchase gift cards
2. **Store** gift cards in digital wallet
3. **Redeem** gift cards via QR code, code, or link
4. **Check** balance and view history

- **Payments:** Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay, UPI
